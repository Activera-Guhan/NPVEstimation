Basic Details
Analysis Options
Overall Summary
i
Always generated. Produces a plain-language written summary: base NPV verdict, P10/P50/P90 range, top risk driver, and confidence level. Useful for client-facing reports.
Cash Flow Diagram
i
Shows annual net cash flows as bars (green = positive, red = negative) and cumulative discounted PV as a line. The line crossing $0 is the break-even point.
Sensitivity Spider Chart
i
Shows how NPV responds as each variable moves ±20% from base. Steeper slope = more sensitive. Use the dropdowns in Results to filter or highlight a variable.
Monte Carlo Distribution
i
Runs 10,000 simulated scenarios with randomly varied inputs. Produces a histogram of NPV outcomes and marks P10 (pessimistic), P50 (median), and P90 (optimistic) values.
Input Options
Initial Investment (dollars)
i
Total upfront project budget ($). Recorded as a negative cash flow at Year 0. e.g. 1000000 for $1M.
Annual Benefit (dollars)
i
Base annual hard benefit at full adoption — e.g. revenue or direct cost savings that appear on a P&L. Enter $/year. Soft benefits are added below.
Time
i
Duration: total project life in years. Implementation: years before benefits begin. Benefit years = Duration − Implementation.
Annual Expenses (dollars)
i
Ongoing annual operating cost (opex) once live. Subtracted from benefits each year. Inflates over time per the inflation rate.
Discount, Tax, Inflation & Overhead Rates
i
All entered as decimals. Discount: WACC / required return (e.g. 0.10). Inflation: general price inflation (e.g. 0.03). Tax: corporate tax (e.g. 0.25). Overhead: indirect cost ratio (e.g. 0.10).
Optional Inputs (click to expand)
1. Project Adoption Ramp Up
i
Models how quickly people actually adopt and use the solution, instead of assuming full benefit starts immediately. Enter how many years after implementation it takes to reach full (100%) adoption — e.g. 2 means benefits ramp up smoothly over the first 2 years of the benefit period, then run at 100% for every year after that. Enter 0 for instant adoption (same result as leaving Adoption Ramp Up unchecked). This value must be no larger than the benefit period (Duration − Implementation years). Leave this unchecked for no adoption period — full benefit starts immediately at 100%.
